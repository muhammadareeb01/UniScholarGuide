MBZUAI Scholarship in UAE 2026

Fully Funded Masters & PhD Opportunities in Artificial Intelligence

Deadline: December 15, 2025

Overview

The MBZUAI Scholarship Program 2026 is an outstanding opportunity for students who want to study in UAE for free. This international scholarship aims at pursuing advanced specific knowledge and skills in artificial intelligence through offering different programs. The top-rated faculty and staff motivate and guides students in making the right choices for their career paths.

The fully-funded Scholarship program is carried out to attract brilliant students to fulfill the institute’s core mission of establishing interdisciplinary and collaborative research and development potential in the field of artificial intelligence.

Host University

Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)

Degree Level

Master’s (2 Years) & PhD (4 Years)

Location

Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (UAE)

Scholarship Benefits

  • 100% Free Tuition Fees
  • Free Accommodation (On-campus)
  • Monthly Stipend (MSc): AED 8,000 - 10,000 (Revised)
  • Monthly Stipend (PhD): AED 10,000 - 15,000 (Revised)
  • Health Insurance & UAE Visa Sponsorship
Note for International Students: The stipend is extremely generous and covers living expenses comfortably in Abu Dhabi.

Available Programs

Scholarships are available for both Master's and PhD degrees in the following specializations:

Computer Science Computer Vision Machine Learning Natural Language Processing Robotics Statistics and Data Science

Eligibility Criteria

For Master's Program

  • Bachelor's degree with a minimum CGPA of 3.2 on a 4.0 scale.
  • English Proficiency: TOEFL iBT (90) or IELTS Academic (6.0) or EmSAT (1550).
  • Statement of Purpose (800 words).
  • Entry Test & Interview.

For Doctoral (PhD) Program

  • Master's degree with a minimum CGPA of 3.2 on a 4.0 scale.
  • English Proficiency: TOEFL iBT (90) or IELTS Academic (6.0) or EmSAT (1550).
  • Comprehensive Research Statement.
  • Statement of Purpose (800 words).

Required Documents

  • Online Application Form
  • CV / Resume
  • Official Transcripts & Degrees
  • Valid Passport Copy
  • Passport Size Photo (White Background)
  • Referee Recommendations (2 for MSc, 3 for PhD)
