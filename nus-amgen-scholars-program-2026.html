Internship Summary

Applications are now invited for the NUS Amgen Scholars Program 2026, a prestigious fully funded summer internship hosted by the National University of Singapore (NUS). This intensive 8-week research program is scheduled to run from June 1, 2026, to July 31, 2026.

Designed for aspiring undergraduate researchers, the program offers a unique opportunity to engage in hands-on biomedical research under the mentorship of world-renowned faculty members. A major highlight of the program is the Amgen Scholars Asia Symposium in Kyoto, Japan (August 3–6, 2026), where participants will present their research findings and network with peers and scientists. The entire experience—including travel to Singapore and Japan, accommodation, and living expenses—is fully funded, with no application fee and no requirement for IELTS if your university's medium of instruction is English.

Financial Benefits

The NUS Amgen Scholars Program provides comprehensive financial support to ensure participants can focus entirely on their research:

  • Stipend: receive SGD 6,000 for the 8-week duration (covers local transport and meals).
  • Accommodation: Fully arranged and covered by NUS.
  • Travel: Round-trip airfare to Singapore and from Japan back to your home country.
  • Symposium Travel: All costs associated with the trip to the Asia Symposium in Japan are covered.
  • Visa: Visa application fees are fully reimbursed.

Internship Areas

Participants will be assigned to research labs in one of the following faculties/departments:

  • Biological Sciences
  • Biochemistry
  • Pharmacology
  • Anatomy
  • Physiology
  • Microbiology & Immunology

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for the program, candidates must meet the following requirements:

  • Enrollment: Must be enrolled in an undergraduate program at any university in Asia.
  • Academic Status: Must have completed at least the first year of undergraduate study.
  • Academic Record: Demonstrate a strong record of academic performance.
  • Language: English proficiency (Letter from university confirmed English instruction is sufficient; IELTS not mandatory).
  • Nationality: Open to all nationals, but must be studying in an eligible Asian country (see list below).

Eligible Countries (Home University Base)

You must be studying at a university located in one of these regions:

  • Eastern Asia: China (including Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan), Japan, Korea (North & South), Mongolia.
  • South-Eastern Asia: Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Indonesia, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Brunei, Timor-Leste.
  • Southern Asia: India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bhutan, Maldives, Afghanistan, Iran.
  • Central Asia: Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan.
  • Western Asia: Turkey, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Cyprus, Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Iraq, Israel, Jordan, Lebanon, Palestine, Syria, Yemen.
