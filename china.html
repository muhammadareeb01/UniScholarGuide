Xiamen University CSC Scholarship 2025

Fully Funded Chinese Government Scholarship for International Students

Deadline: Feb 15, 2025

Overview

Xiamen University CSC Scholarship 2025 is offered by the Chinese Government Scholarship Program to promote Chinese higher education on an international level. Xiamen University (XMU) is one of the reputable universities assigned to select foreign students for studying in degree programs under this fully funded scholarship program in China.

Students will have the option to study their educational degrees either in Chinese or English medium. XMU has developed an integrated and diverse educational system that produces graduates with exceptional skills. Renowned researchers and educators come together here to introduce new and innovative ideas for global advancement.

Host University

Xiamen University (XMU)

Programs

Bachelor’s, Master’s & Doctoral (PhD)

Location

Xiamen, China

Scholarship Benefits

  • Full Tuition Fee Waiver
  • Free University Dormitory (or accommodation subsidy)
  • Stipend (Bachelor's): CNY 2,500 / month
  • Stipend (Master's): CNY 3,000 / month
  • Stipend (PhD): CNY 3,500 / month
  • Medical Insurance (CNY 800/year included)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants typically must be non-Chinese citizens in good health.
  • For Bachelor’s: High school diploma, under 25 years old.
  • For Master’s: Bachelor's degree, excellent academic record, under 35 years old.
  • For Doctoral: Master's degree, excellent academic record, under 40 years old.
  • Language: HSK Level 4 for Chinese-taught programs. English proficiency for English-taught programs.
  • Must not be holding any other Chinese Government Scholarship.

How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

  1. Apply for admission to Xiamen University academic programs approved by CSC. (Note: Pre-acceptance letter required for grad students).
  2. Complete the online application on the CSC Portal (studyinchina.csc.edu.cn).
  3. Fill in the application, enter the correct Agency Number for XMU, and upload required documents.
  4. Download and sign the CSC Application Form.
  5. Upload the signed CSC form and other documents to the XMU Application System (application.xmu.edu.cn).
  6. Pay any required application fee (if applicable by XMU).

Required Documents

  • CSC Application Form
  • Passport Copy & Photo
  • Highest Diploma & Transcripts (Notarized)
  • Study Plan / Research Proposal
  • Two Recommendation Letters
  • Language Proficiency Certificate
  • Physical Examination Form
  • Non-Criminal Record Certificate
