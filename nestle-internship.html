Nestle Internships 2026 Overview

To all the passionate students and recent graduates, do you want to gain early career experience in the world’s largest food & Beverage company? Applications are now open for the NESTLE Internships 2026. Nestle has announced multiple internship positions for its offices worldwide, seeking talented and motivated students from around the world.

The duration of the internship is six months, offering hands-on corporate experience under the supervision of Nestlé staff. Opportunities are available in locations such as Spain, Italy, the United States, Malaysia, Belgium, the Netherlands, and more.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Nestlé internship, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Current bachelor’s and master’s students in relevant fields.
  • Undergraduate students must be in the 2nd or 3rd year of their degree program.
  • Applicants who have completed their degree program in the last 12 months.
  • Applicants must hold an excellent academic record.
  • Must be available to attend the complete program.
  • Applicants must be authorized to work in the country where the internship is based.

Competencies

  • Excellent communication & Interpersonal skills.
  • Actively engaged in extracurricular activities.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel.
  • Demonstrate interest in working with Nestlé.
  • Willing to work in a multicultural work environment.
  • Demonstrate passion and adaptability to work collaboratively in a team.
  • Proficiency in the English language, both spoken and written.

Benefits

  • Nestle offers competitive stipend for the program duration.
  • Gain hands-on experience while working on real-time projects.
  • Mentorship and career guidance from industry experts.
  • Official certification upon completion of the program.
  • Networking opportunities with global peers and industry experts.
  • Gain valuable insights into the world’s leading organization.
  • Work flexibility offers both remote and in-person internships.

How to Apply?

The application process is online, via the official Nestlé career portal. Individuals interested in the internship need to visit the official link below, search for the available internship positions, select the position that matches their interests, and complete the application form.
