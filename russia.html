Scholarship Overview

Russia offers two major fully funded scholarship pathways for international students: the Russian Government Scholarship (Education-in-Russia) and the Open Doors Russian Scholarship Project. These programs cover tuition fees for Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD degrees at top Russian universities.

With world-class education in Engineering, Medicine, IT, and Sciences, Russia is a top destination for students seeking quality education without the financial burden.

Open Doors (Olympiad)

Best for Master's & PhD. Selection based on portfolio and online tests. Winners get early admission.

Deadline: Dec 2025 (Approx)

Govt Scholarship

For Bachelor's, Master's, & PhD. Applied via Education-in-Russia portal. Standard selection process.

Deadline: Jan - Feb 2026

Scholarship Benefits

  • 100% Tuition Fee Waiver (Full Degree)
  • Monthly Stipend (Varies by University)
  • Subsidized Accommodation (Dormitories)
  • Preparatory Russian Year (Free if needed)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality: International students from all countries (Non-Russian citizens).
  • Education:
    • Bachelor's: High School Diploma / 12th Grade.
    • Master's: Bachelor's Degree.
    • PhD: Master's Degree.
  • Grades: Good academic record (At least 70% recommended).
  • Health: Medically fit (HIV/AIDS, Hep B/C, TB Negative).
  • Passport: Valid for at least 18 months from arrival.

Required Documents

  • Educational Documents (Transcripts & Degrees)
  • Valid Passport Copy
  • Medical Certificate & HIV Report
  • Motivation Letter
  • Recommendation Letters (for Master's/PhD)
