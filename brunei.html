Government of Brunei Darussalam Scholarship 2026-27

Fully Funded Diploma, Undergraduate & Masters Opportunities

Deadline: 15 February 2026

Overview

The Government of Brunei Darussalam Scholarship is a prestigious fully funded opportunity for international students who wish to study in Brunei at top institutions such as Universiti Brunei Darussalam (UBD) and other recognized universities. This scholarship covers tuition fees, on-campus accommodation, round-trip airfare, visa costs, meals, books, and provides a generous living allowance. With English widely used in academic and daily life, Brunei offers a comfortable and welcoming environment for students from around the world.

Country

Brunei Darussalam

Study Level

Diploma, Bachelor, Masters

Provider

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Scholarship Benefits

  • Tuition Fees Exemption: Full coverage of tuition and compulsory fees.
  • Airfare: One return economy class air-ticket (start and end of program).
  • Monthly Allowance: BND 500.00 personal allowance.
  • Allowances: Food (BND 150/month), Books (BND 600/year).
  • Accommodation: Provided at residential college.
  • Medical: Outpatient treatment at government hospitals.
  • Baggage: Up to BND 500 (Non-ASEAN) / BND 250 (ASEAN) upon completion.

Participating Universities

Applicants can apply to the following higher education institutions:

  • Universiti Brunei Darussalam (UBD)
  • Universiti Islam Sultan Sharif Ali (UNISSA)
  • Universiti Teknologi Brunei (UTB)
  • Kolej Universiti Perguruan Ugama Seri Begawan (KUPU SB)
  • Politeknik Brunei (PB)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit:
    • Undergraduate/Diploma: Not exceeding 25 years old.
    • Postgraduate Master’s: Not exceeding 35 years old.
    • (Age as of 1st July of the year the course starts).
  • Nationality: International students (Bruneians and Permanent Residents are NOT eligible).
  • English Proficiency: Credit 6 at GCE ‘O’ Level, Grade ‘C’ in IGCSE English (2nd language), IELTS 6.0, or TOEFL 550.
  • Applicants must typically be studying in Brunei for the first time.

Required Documents

  • Recent passport size photograph.
  • Certified true copies of academic qualifications (certificates, transcripts).
  • Certified English translations of supporting documents.
  • Certified true copies of Birth Certificate and Passport.
  • References and recommendations (C1 and C2 forms).
