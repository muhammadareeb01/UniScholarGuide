Essential Information

What is this Korean Scholarship?

In Korea, most of the scholarships are offered by professors. Professors hire you in the lab, and you work on company projects. Projects are based on research and development of products, etc. of companies. Therefore, your tuition fee and living expenses are paid by the professors using the money of the companies.

Why apply early?

The Korean universities will start taking admissions soon for Spring 2026. Students from different countries try to contact and impress professors earlier than the opening of the admission process. Therefore, if you apply late, the professor might have already chosen a student for their lab. It is highly recommended to start finding and contacting professors now to secure your seat.

How to Apply!

  1. Find Professors: Search relevant professors using Google with keywords like University Name + your major + Korea. Example: Sookmyung Chemistry Korea.
  2. Contact Them: Email professors with a Cover Letter and PDF Research CV.
  3. Wait for Response: If a professor replies positively, apply for admission to the university.
  4. English Proficiency: While many universities don't require IELTS/TOEFL, having an English Proficiency Certificate is helpful.

Note: Don’t ask Korean professors for an "Acceptance Letter" (like in China). Just ask if they have a position in their lab.
